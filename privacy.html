Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

At Brum App, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our expiration tracking service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address via Supabase Authentication.
  • Document Data: We store information you provide about your documents, such as names, types (e.g., ID Card, Insurance), and expiration dates.
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you alerts before documents expire, we collect an anonymized push token for your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account and provide access to your data.
  • To calculate expiration statuses and generate notifications.
  • To sync your data across your devices using Supabase as our backend provider.

3. Data Security and Third-Party Services

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides industry-standard encryption and security measures. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time directly through the Brum App interface. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, you can do so through the mobile app settings or via our Account Deletion Request page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at awt.apps.dev@gmail.com.
